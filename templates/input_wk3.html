Reimbursement Rate Predictor

How much will your ER bill be reimbursed?

The Reimbursement Rate Predictor is a machine learning algorithm helping providers and patients to estimate the reimbursement rate of an ER visit. After submitting the claim form, you will get a estimated percentage of how much this bill will be covered by the payer, and a list of important features of the claim that affect the reimbursement rate the most.
